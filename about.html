Who We Are

We are a collective of game developers living and operating in the Tri-Cities region of Northeast Tennessee. We run the gamut from freelancers, to students, to hobbyists and everything in between. We are open to people at all levels of experience, skills and interest. Hop onto our discord server and introduce yourself!

What We Do

We attend conferences, conventions, and expos. We engage in panels, presentations, and live demonstrations. We also do game jams, group projects, and semi-regular meetups and hangouts. The group is a mix of professional development and social engagement, so our events run the gamut from seminars to bar-side chats.
